Digraph

Keep track of thousands of articles and blog posts. Find anything you've spent more than a minute or two reading.

How it works

Digraph allows you to categorize links under one or more topics, and topics can be as specific as you want. Think of a topic or phrase that you might look for when trying to find a link. The next time you search for the link, if the original topic wasn't helpful, recategorize the link under whatever group of topics and phrases came to mind during the search.

Specific topics and phrases can be placed under more general topics and phrases. Subtopics can belong to more than one parent topic.

Digraph is different from browser bookmark managers in that it does not assume that the number of links you'll want to keep around will be in the tens or hundreds. By making it easy to build out a large collection of topics, Digraph allows you to keep track of thousands and even tens of thousands of links.

Getting started

Log in with Github to get started. You can also take a look at some of the links and topics that have been added to the public collection here. Links and topics can be private or public.
